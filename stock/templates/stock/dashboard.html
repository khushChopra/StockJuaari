Introducing StockJuaari


The stock market goes up and down, but savvy investors come out ahead over the long term. You don't need to be a hedge-fund manager or a billionaire. An ordinary individual investor can beat Wall Street at its own game. You just need to have information that gives you an edge. That's where StockJuaari come in. We provide stock predictions that can beat the market. Imagine where you will be 10 years from now if you start paying attention to our stock forecasts.


The results may seem like magic, but there is sophisticated math behind them. First, we harness the collective intelligence of online investors. Unlike others, we don't stop there. The data we collect is just the raw material for our complex analyses. Our founders, sophomores at Computer Science, IIT (BHU) Varanasi, create algorithms that are able to mine our huge collection of data to determine which of our collective predictions are most likely to be accurate and should be given the most weight.


These two layers — collecting large quantities of useful data and ranking and analysing them in subtle ways — combine to produce a model with the power to make market- beating predictions. With StockJuaari, you don't have to choose between the collective wisdom of the crowds or the expertise of the exceptional investors. We present you with actionable information from both, filtered through our algorithms that collect the data that has the strongest predictive power.
